*Caramelized French Toast*

Japanese Soft Custard Pudding is a very soft and delicate pudding made out of cream, milk, egg yolk and sugar. It is easy to make and an amazing dessert to go with your tea when friends and families are over.

  Ingredients (1 Serving)

French Toast
- 80~100ml of Milk
- 1 Egg
- 1 tablespoon of Sugar
- 1 slice of super thick bread
-Butter
Caramel
- 1 tablespoon of Sugar

    1. Making the French Toast
      1. Spread as much Cream cheese as you like on one slice of bread and sandwich it with the other slice of bread
        *Add sugar to the cream cheese to sweeten it if you like
      2. Mix the egg and Late very well and pour into a ziplock
      3. Put step 1 into the ziplock and sit it for a few hours in the refrigeratour untill step 2 has soaked into the sandwich
      4. Using a lid, toast both sides of the sandwich on a pan untill you see a nice burn
    2. Adding the Topping
      1. Add some Cocoa powder and gently wipped Creamon top and injoy your Tiramisu Freanch Toast!
